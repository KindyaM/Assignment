About us

Background

Mercier Bank was founded in 2013 as a small bank that granted personalized financial services. Over the years, the bank has expanded globally, reaching over 10 million customers in 20 countries worldwide. The bank applies advanced technology to offer smooth customer service at the highest level. Being responsible for its impact on society, the bank actively promotes green initiatives and financial literacy around the globe.

Mission

Empowering people and businesses through innovative, secure, and accessible financial solutions, with the intent of earning and maintaining their trust for sustainable growth.

Values

To be the most trusted financial institution globally in leading the way in financial inclusion and digital transformation while contributing positively to communities and the environment.
